About Us

About Us

Midlands Muttz was set up in 2022 by a group of veterinary nurses who wanted to use their backgrounds in animal behaviour and science to facilitate pet owners in the midlands who needed their precious pets taken care of while they returned to work post-covid. The team is currently made up of 30 walkers/pet-sitters, with further recruitment taking place as the service expands.
